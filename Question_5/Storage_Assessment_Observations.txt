QUESTION 5 - BRIEF OBSERVATIONS (per command)

lsblk
  - Lists block storage devices and their mount points.
  - Shows device name, size and whether read-only/removable.
  - Identifies the main disk holding the root filesystem.

mount | grep '^/dev'
  - Shows which devices are mounted and their filesystem type.
  - Confirms root is ext4 and mounted read-write.
  - Read-only mounts cannot be modified at runtime.

df -h
  - Reports filesystem size, used and available space.
  - '-h' gives human-readable units (G, M).
  - Use% highlights how full each filesystem is.

df -i
  - Reports inode usage instead of block/space usage.
  - A filesystem can run out of inodes even with free space.
  - Low IUse% means plenty of inodes remain.

vi Storage_Assessment_Report.txt
  - Opens the report file in the vi editor.
  - 'i' inserts text, Esc exits insert mode.
  - ':wq' saves and quits the file.
